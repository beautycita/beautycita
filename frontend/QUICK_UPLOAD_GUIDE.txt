UPLOAD STEPS:
━━━━━━━━━━━━━
Step 1: Go to Google Play Console
        → https://play.google.com/console

Step 2: Navigate to your app
        → Release → Production (or Testing)

Step 3: Create new release
        → Click "Create new release"

Step 4: Upload APP BUNDLE
        → Drag/drop beautycita-v1.1-final.aab
        → Or click "Upload" and select the file

Step 5: Upload MAPPING FILE
        → Look for section "Deobfuscation files" or "ProGuard"
        → Click "Upload" or "Add file"
        → Select beautycita-v1.1-mapping.txt

Step 6: Add Release Notes
        → Copy from GOOGLE_PLAY_RELEASE_NOTES.txt
        → Or use this short version:

        Enhanced privacy policy with 2025 compliance, 
        1-day support response time, optimized with R8/ProGuard

Step 7: Add Test Instructions
        → Copy from: /var/www/beautycita.com/
          GOOGLE_PLAY_CONSOLE_INSTRUCTIONS.txt

Step 8: Privacy Policy
        → URL: https://beautycita.com/privacy/play-store

Step 9: Review & Submit
        → Double-check everything
        → Click "Submit for review"
